Orange-Apple Sparkler

This very simple drink is refreshing on a hot summer's eve. You can easily double or tripe the recipe to serve a crowd.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup orange juice concentrate
  • 2 cups sparkling cider
  • 2 cups sparkling water
  • Crushed or cracked ice
  • Orange slices

Instructions

  1. Place the juice concentrate in a pitcher (if it's still frozen, stir until liquefied).
  2. Stir in the cider and sparkling water.
  3. Fill tall glasses with the ice.
  4. Add the cider mixture.
  5. Garnish with orange slices.
