Fiesta Dip

Sweet red peppers and shredded cucumbers add crunch to this low-fat offering that features several Mediterranean favorite foods: red peppers, garlic, and thyme. Serve with pita wedges, low-fat crackers or crisp raw vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup shredded cucumbers
  • 1 cup nonfat sour cream
  • 1/2 cup minced sweet red peppers
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme

Instructions

  1. Squeeze all excess moisture from the cucumbers.
  2. Place the cucumbers in a small bowl.
  3. Stir in the sour cream, peppers, garlic, and thyme.
  4. Transfer to a serving bowl and chill until needed.
