Mango Smoothie

Mangos and bananas have a rich, creamy texture with only barest trace of fat. That makes them good choices for low-fat shakes. Mangos are available from January through August. Buy fruit that's got a yellow or reddish-yellow skin.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (8 ounces) juice-packed pineapple chunks
  • 1 large rip mango, peeled and chopped
  • 1 ripe banana sliced
  • 1 cup nonfat frozen vanilla yogurt
  • crushed or cracked ice

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the pineapple (with juice), mangoes, and bananas.
  2. Process until smooth.
  3. Add the frozen yogurt.
  4. Blend well.
  5. With the blender running, gradually drop in enough ice to bring the level up to four cups.
  6. Blend until ice is pureed
